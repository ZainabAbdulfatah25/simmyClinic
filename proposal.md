# Project Proposal, Timeline & Commercial Agreement

**Project Title:** SimmyCare Consultation Website Development  
**Prepared for:** Client (SimmyCare Online Clinic)  
**Prepared by:** Nexel Technologies  
**Role:** Website Development Services  
**Date:** June 18, 2026  
**Agreed Tier:** **Standard Package** (₦250,000 - ₦350,000)

---

## 1. Project Overview
This proposal presents the development of a hospital consultation website designed to improve patient access to healthcare services through a structured and user-friendly digital platform. The system will enable patients to book consultations online, view available doctors, and communicate efficiently with the hospital through integrated digital channels.

---

## 2. Project Scope & Features (Standard Package)

### 👥 Patient-Facing Features
- **Hospital Homepage & Service Overview:** A professional, responsive landing page detailing services, clinic hours, and specialties.
- **Doctor Profiles & Specializations:** Listings of available medical professionals with their areas of expertise, qualifications, and schedules.
- **Online Appointment Booking System:** A streamlined, public-facing booking flow allowing patients to request and schedule appointments.
- **Contact & Inquiry Form:** Simple feedback and communication channel for general queries.
- **WhatsApp Integration:** Floating chat widget for direct communication with clinic personnel.
- **Mobile-Responsive Design:** Fully optimized interface for smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers.

### 🛡️ Admin Panel
- **Manage Appointments:** View, confirm, reschedule, or cancel booking requests.
- **Doctor Profile Management:** Add, update, or remove doctor profiles and schedules.
- **Inquiry Management:** Read, track, and manage patient inquiries from the contact form.
- **Basic Dashboard Overview:** Simplified dashboard showing key stats (e.g., total appointments, recent inquiries, active doctors).

### 🚫 Future Enhancements (Out of Scope for current Phase)
- 🎥 Video consultations
- 💳 Online payment gateway integrations
- 🔐 Patient login/registration system (accounts)
- ✉️ Automated Email/SMS notification dispatchers
